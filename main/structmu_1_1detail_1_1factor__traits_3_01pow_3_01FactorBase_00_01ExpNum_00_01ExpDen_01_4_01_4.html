Detailed Description

template<factor FactorBase, std::intmax_t ExpNum, std::intmax_t ExpDen>
struct mu::detail::factor_traits< pow< FactorBase, ExpNum, ExpDen > >

If the base of a pow matches the factor concept, then the resulting pow is also a factor.

The exponent of the resulting factor is modified.
