Detailed Description

Concept evaluates true when FromUnits and ToUnits are exactly equivalent.

That is, the conversion from FromUnits to ToUnits is exactly 1. This may be the case even if the units are derived from different expressions. Equivalent units are convertible by definition.

Examples of equivalent units:

  1. units_equivalent_to<meters, meters>
  2. units_equivalent_to<newton_meters, watt_seconds>

Examples of units that are NOT equivalent:

  1. units_equivalent_to<meters, feet>
  2. units_equivalent_to<meters, square_meters>
